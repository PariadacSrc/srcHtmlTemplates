OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Traction, our philosophy consists in doing everything to help you stay on the road. We understand that your time is precious – that is why we take care of everything when it comes to heavy vehicle parts, so you can focus on managing your company and keeping your fleet on the road. Whether it is to provide you with the right parts at the right time, relevant information or technical support, or simply to visit your location – we are always by your side, ready to give you a hand.

PRODUCTS

Traction has a long track record of innovation in the Canadian replacement parts market. We were the first in our field to provide numerous new product lines, and new programs especially designed to make our customers' lives easier. Our objective is to go beyond the sale of heavy vehicle parts by providing you with comprehensive business solutions that truly meet your needs.

We are your one-stop shop for all your needs in truck and trailer parts and accessories.
